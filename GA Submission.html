About Me

My name is Anthony Guariglia, and I am an electronics engineer working in the greater Boston area. I completed my education at Northeastern University, earning a B.S. in Electrical Engineering in May of 2020. Since then, I've continued my work at MKS Instruments, designing and sustaining the electronics for precision throttle valves used in various applications. Having worked at various companies across the robotics, consumer-audio, fitness-tech, and now semiconductor markets, I have a well-rounded appreciation and acute interest in the various applications of modern electronics. I'm also passionate about music and am an avid golfer and snowboarder, and enjoy any occasion in which I can spend time outdoors with friends.
